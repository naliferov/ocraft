Two senses:

  1. as the data of our sensations
  2. as a method of multiple (experimental) re-checks

Kant himself had no doubt about the existence of a priori knowledge. No experience can lend knowledge universality and necessity, that is, the force of a law.

Space

According to Newton — absolute, independent of things. According to Leibniz — extension as a property of things. The principle of incongruent counterparts.

Kant proposed a solution to the paradox of space: a priori forms of sensibility — time and space. They are neither properties of things nor an absolute container, but the forms in which we perceive.

Understanding and Reason

Understanding — that by means of which things are thought.

Forms of the understanding (categories): quality, relation (affirmation/negation), modality, quantitative characteristic.

Reason — not the understanding. It possesses a directing and consolidating power. It consists of three regulative ideas whose purpose is to regulate the work of the understanding:

  • God — the possible meaning of the world
  • Soul — the consciousness of the subject
  • World — the world as a whole

Thanks to them, scattered pieces of understanding-based knowledge are ordered into a coherent picture.
