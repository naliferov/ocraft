Start here

Openclipart + SVG Repo (CC0, no attribution, infinite raw material) and unDraw / Humaaans (recolorable scenes & people to recombine).

Tip: SVGs are just XML — drop several into one <svg> canvas, recolor via fill, reposition with <g transform> instead of an editor.
