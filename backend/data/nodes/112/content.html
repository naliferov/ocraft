Determines the shape of an aircraft's components in order to optimize lift, reduce drag, and improve performance Studies airflows and the forces acting on the aircraft

  • they consider not the flight itself, but the airflow around the craft against the oncoming air
  • Weight - Lift
  • Drag - Thrust
  • Lift depends on wing shape, surface area, aircraft speed, angle of attack
  • Bernoulli's principle - faster flow - lower pressure
  • Boundary layer (laminar, turbulent)
  • Reynolds number - tells whether the flow is calm or chaotic.
  • Low number → viscosity wins → flow is laminar, High - inertia wins - turbulent eddies, swirls, chaos
  • Mach number - how many times the object's speed is greater (or less) than the speed of sound
