books: no single book directly stages **G-code family vs expression family**  but the space between them is covered well by four:

\- **Martin Fowler - Domain-Specific Languages**

\- Jon Bentley, "Little Languages" (the classic essay, in More Programming Pearls) — the founding text of the "tiny notation for one job" tradition vlang belongs to

\- Kernighan & Pike, The Practice of Programming, ch. 9 "Notation" — little languages from the people who built awk/pic; closest in spirit to what you're doing

\- Michael Scott, Programming Language Pragmatics — the heavyweight survey if you want the full family tree (imperative/expression/declarative/stack)
