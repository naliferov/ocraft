Play, don't complete. The playing is the point

Inefficiency is the rebellion. Wander. Waste time.
Don't make fun a duty. Refuse the command to "Enjoy!"
Don't rebuild work (grind, dailies, 100%) inside the game.
Treat games as art — study the world, decode the intent.
Solve creatively; don't bug-skip or exploit.
Drop bad games; go deep on good ones.
Resist the Share button, leaderboards, streamer-watching.
Be a participant, not a spectator. Bring back couch co-op.
Lose the loot, lose the fear. Play like a kid.
