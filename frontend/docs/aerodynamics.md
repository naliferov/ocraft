aerodynamics — shapes aircraft components to optimize lift, reduce drag, improve performance; studies airflow & forces on aircraft  
analyzes airflow around craft vs oncoming air, not flight itself  
weight ↔ lift  
drag ↔ thrust  
lift depends on wing shape, surface area, speed, angle of attack  
bernoulli's principle — faster flow → lower pressure  
boundary layer (laminar, turbulent)  
reynolds number — tells if flow is calm or chaotic  
low → viscosity wins → **laminar** ; high → inertia wins → turbulent (eddies, swirls, chaos)  
mach number — how many times object's speed is above/below speed of sound
