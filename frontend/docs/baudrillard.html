everything strives to be realized more and more fully. One day the social will be realized completely - there will be no social isolation left. One day everything will become culturalized, everything, every object, will be a so-called aesthetic object, and then nothing at all will be an aesthetic object anymore.

nullity is a mysterious quality to which not just anyone or anything can lay claim. insignificance, genuine nonsense - this is a challenge, a victorious challenge to meaning, to the poverty of meaning. the art of the disappearance of meaning is an exceptional quality, inherent only in a few of the rarest works, which made no such claim at all

contemporary art plays on this uncertainty and indeterminacy, on this impossibility of any evaluative judgment regarding its aesthetic foundations, and speculates on the guilt of those who understand nothing in it, or who fail to understand that there is nothing to understand here
