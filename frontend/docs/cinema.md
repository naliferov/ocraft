**movie: wet eyes**  
**pitch** : lonely young man escapes isolation by pulling women into emotionally dangerous games of silence, tension, intimacy  
then someone appears who can withstand his mode of contact → the bond becomes "metaphysical"  
  
**protagonist, him**  
parents divorced; lives alone; parents in different countries  
aggressive & reserved  
wants to be the subject of his life, not the object of circumstances, loneliness, the body, decay  
inside: feeling, desire, tenderness, sexuality, impulse toward contact, thought, self-image — but a barrier between inner & outer  
not fully silent: writes messages, makes contact, looks for girls at social events, reaches out — but feels he doesn't express fully, only a weak part of the inside reaches out  
wants real contact but can't withstand the moment it becomes real — anxiety surges, all his demons start ringing  
hidden desire: sudden direct experience of reality w/o the inner barrier — world stops feeling separate, constant self-observation disappears, "i can't manifest myself" disappears, for the first time he's simply present  
broken but alive: humor, strange dignity, energy, turns his decay into performance — cracks jokes at the wrong moment, plays strange games, aestheticizes pain, turns loneliness into mythology  
  
**protagonist, her**  
she: mirror, catalyst; next to her his inner world trembles, opens up  
the only one who feels his intensity — or fears it → she becomes central to the theme
